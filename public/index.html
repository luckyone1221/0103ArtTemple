Who we are

We are Ukrainians who cannot stay away from the genocide taking place in our country.

arTemple is a 100% non-profit initiative aimed at supporting people who have suffered from a terrible war happening in Ukraine right now as you’re reading this message.

Our mission and goals

In arTemple, talented Ukrainian people are opening their souls to the world through drawings, illustrations, photos, videos, animations, music, poems, 3D and more. If these Ukrainian souls resonate with you and if you want to save them from obliteration, join our initiative before it’s too late.

Help our people

Millions of people in Ukraine are suffering from war and they need a helping hand today. Warm housing, food, water, medicines, emergency treatment, safer areas - together with you we can provide all these things to those who are in a desparate need.

Unite the world

Сompassionate people from all over the world are eager to help the Ukrainians. arTemple is created to allow anybody to provide their help by experiencing our culture, feelings, struggles and victories.

Share the truth

arTmeple is created at the epicenter of war. The world needs to see the ugly truth to learn the peace lesson. And we are here to show the truth.
